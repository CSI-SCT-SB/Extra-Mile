About Saraswati Sewa Foundation

Saraswati Seva Foundation (SSF) is a fast-growing non-profit organization with a vision to reduce poverty and encourage social justice by educating the children, enabling the youth, empowering the women and supporting the farmers. SSF partners with other development agencies and institutions, and promotes grass-root change leadership in the remote regions. We celebrate their championing of social causes and support them using our resource base, knowledge tools and networks of institutions..

Saraswati Seva Foundation (SSF) is committed to serve the under-privileged communities using knowledge tools. Our effort is to work towards building technology-driven and scalable solutions for specific social needs which are environmentally sustainable and commercially viable.

SSF aims to develop resilience and well-being by capacity building, economic engagement, empowering the underprivileged communities and women with skills and information.

Our Vision

SSF’s vision is to cause and enable sustainable social change by reducing poverty, inequality and encouraging social justice by educating the children, enabling youth through employment generation, empowering women and supporting the farmers. We want to be the platform and an actor to enable, encourage and inspire a sustainable, harmonious and inclusive change in society and the environment.

Our Mission

Democratize the social and economic benefits and make the bottom 250 million people work wonders for themselves and the community.

Farmers

Indian agriculture employs the largest share of the workforce (42%) though its share in overall gross domestic product (GDP) is only 16.5 percent. SSF is catalyzing a transformative change in India’s agriculture by offering FPO support, Agri CoE and Agri startups.

SSF provides a multi – stakeholder platform to support institutional development (Agri startups) and business stabilization in rural areas.

Children

Children are our greatest resource and the biggest hope. Much like water, they are ready to be shaped up as the mould presents itself.

SSF’s programs for children ensure that children in neglected regions and under resourced schools have access to best breed educational content and gain critical life skills alongside 21st century knowledge to prepare them for an ever changing world

SSF aims to bridge educational and digital divides through technology adoption.

Youth

Youth is not only an asset from the perspective of demographic dividend in India but also an important driver of technology-led evolution.

SSF assists in developing fundamental skills that are complementary to emerging technologies such as literacy, basic academic skills – together with entrepreneurial, financial, digital, cognitive skills (STEM), understanding that the future lies in workers creating economic value with machines rather than against them.
